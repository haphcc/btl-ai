# 1. H thng tn ch theo quy nh ca Vit Nam v mt s quc gia Chu u

## 1.1. H thng tn ch ca Vit Nam
Thng t s 17/2021/TT-BGDT ca B Gio dc v o to quy nh v h thng tn ch ti Vit Nam nh sau:
- Tn ch c s dng  tnh khi lng hc tp ca sinh vin.
- 01 tn ch c tnh tng ng 50 gi hc tp nh mc ca ngi hc, bao gm c thi gian d gi ging, gi hc c hng dn, t hc, nghin cu, tri nghim v d kim tra, nh gi.
- i vi hot ng dy trn lp, 01 tn ch yu cu thc hin ti thiu 15 gi ging dy hoc 30 gi thc hnh, th nghim, bi tp, tho lun.

## 1.2. H thng chuyn i v tch ly tn ch Chu u ti mt s quc gia Chu u
- Theo H thng chuyn i v tch ly tn ch Chu u (European Credit Transfer and Accumulation System - ECTS), cc trng i hc tham gia tin trnh Bolognal p dng ECTS  tnh khi lng hc tp ca sinh vin v so snh khi lng hc tp gia cc chng trnh o to nhm chuyn i, cng nhn kt qu hc tp ca sinh vin trong cc chng trnh trao i hc tp gia cc trng thnh vin v theo h thng o to ca cc quc gia khc nhau.
- Cn c hng dn ca h thng chuyn i v tch ly tn ch ti Chu u, ty thuc vo quc gia, 01 ECTS c th tng ng trung bnh t 25 gi n 30 gi hc tp trn lp, thc hnh, th nghim, thc tp, t hc. C th:
- Ti o,  v Ty Ban Nha: 1 ECTS = 25 gi hc
- Ti Phn Lan: 1 ECTS = 27 gi hc
- Ti H Lan, B o Nha: 1 ECTS = 28 gi hc
- Ti Cng ha lin bang (CHLB) c, Hungary: 1 ECTS = 30 gi hc

# GUIDELINES FOR CONVERTING STUDY CREDITS BETWEEN BANKING ACADEMY OF VIETNAM AND EUROPEAN CREDIT TRANSFER AND ACCUMULATION SYSTEM (ECTS) IN SOME EUROPEAN COUNTRIES
*Accompanied by Decision No. 2862/QD-HVNH dated July 01st, 2024, of the President of Banking Academy of Vietnam*

## 1. The credit system according to the regulations of Vietnam and some European countries

### 1.1 Vietnamese credits
Circular No. 17/2021/TT-BGDĐT by the Ministry of Education and Training regulates the credit system in Vietnam as follows:
- Credits are used to calculate the student's studying volume.
- 01 credit is equivalent to 50 hours of standard learning time for the student, including theoretical lecture attendance, guided study hours, self-study, research, experiential learning, and time for tests and evaluations.
- For classroom activities, one credit requires a minimum of 15 hours of lecturing or 30 hours of practice, experimentation, and discussion.

### 1.2 European Credit Transfer and Accumulation System-ECTS in some European countries
- According to the European Credit Transfer and Accumulation System (ECTS), the universities participating in the Bologna process apply ECTS to calculate the student workload and compare the student workload with other programs in order to convert and recognize students' learning outcomes in exchange programs at membership institutions and under the systems of different countries.
- According to ECTS Users' Guide, depending on each country, 01 ECTS credit is equal to between 25 to 30 study hours of classroom study, practice, experiment, internship, self-study:
    - In Austria, Italy and Spain: 01 ECTS = 25 study hours
    - In Finland: 01 ECTS = 27 study hours
    - In the Netherlands, Portugal: 01 ECTS = 28 study hours
    - In Germany, Hungary: 01 ECTS = 30 study hours
